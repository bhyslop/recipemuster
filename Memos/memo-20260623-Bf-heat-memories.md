# ₣Bf Heat-Memories — drained paddock deliberation

*Memo — opened 2026-06-23 — provenance home for ideas drained from the ₣Bf (rbk-12-mvp-federation-evolution) paddock as the heat progresses. Experimental practice; see "The drain gate" below.*

## Purpose

The ₣Bf paddock is a holding catalog of federation-evolution ideas. As the heat
progresses, ideas get dispositioned — built into a completed pace, declined, or
superseded — and their deliberation (the named tension, the leans, the options
weighed) no longer belongs in a paddock meant to hold the *shape of remaining
work*. This memo is where that dispositioned deliberation drains, so the paddock
stays focused without the *why* being lost.

Provenance, never authority (the standing memo rule). A drained idea is historical
record: read it freely; if its concern must return, it is re-deliberated into a
fresh pace or spec — never lifted from here as authority.

## What drains here — and what does NOT

Drain ONLY dispositioned ideas:
- **Built** — graduated into a pace that completed; the deliberation is spent and the result lives in code or spec.
- **Declined** — weighed and rejected; recorded with what was considered and why it was set aside.
- **Superseded** — overtaken by a later decision; recorded with the prior framing and what replaced it.

Do NOT drain **deferred-but-still-live** ideas — they stay in the paddock (or move
to an itch / the horizon roadmap if thin). A live idea drained here is a live idea
quietly buried; that is the failure mode to watch.

## The drain gate (experimental, 2026-06-23)

Movement is wrap-considered and groom-judged: when a pace wraps, its wrap decision
considers whether the now-completed work renders some paddock material
dispositioned — but a fact drains here ONLY if a *terminal groom* estimates the
paddock no longer needs it. The groom's judgment, not the wrap alone, releases the
drain.

This practice is being tried and is not obviously going to work well. Revisit if it
adds friction without keeping the paddock clean — or graduate it to a general JJK
practice if it proves out.

## First expected drain

The post-renaming paddock-refocus: once the naming paces land, drain the ₣BZ
lineage and the now-released ₣BZ gating, the dingleberry doctrine, and any
built-or-declined idea — de-staling (not draining) references that now resolve to
existing work.

### foedus, the configured-federation noun — ADOPTED (drained 2026-06-25)

The keystone-vocabulary deliberation: the civic vocabulary named every actor and container in the federation story but had no noun for the configured trust itself — referred to mechanically (pool + provider + mapping) or with the overloaded word "federation."
The noun was the keystone because the healthy payor-governor model is only sayable as a relationship between named nouns: the payor founds one foedus; the payor sanctions a set as eligible; the governor affiliates a depot with a sanctioned foedus; a depot draws its citizens from the foedus it is affiliated with.
Chosen noun: foedus — the Latin treaty between a sovereign and an external people (the foederati), the literal root of "federation"; it pluralizes foedera.
It is a folio, not a colophon (a named foedus is data passed as a parameter to operations homed by actor), which dissolved the worry that a configured-federation tabtarget family would collide with Crucible's rbw-c and ruled out the con-/com- binding-together words (concordat, covenant).
Tradeoff carried to the mint: foedus is Latin and opaque, so the fair-faced cold-probe was the load-bearing gate.
ADOPTED in-heat (operator decision 260623): foedus is the RBS0 civic quoin (rbtf_ federation-civics category) for the configured federation; the grep gate is clean and the cold-probe is discharged by a first-contact gloss.
Verb-register settled: affiance/jilt stay as the founding/un-founding verbs and coexist with the foedus noun (affiance founds ONE foedus per call); the betrothal-register impurity is accepted rather than buy a costly eviction sweep of two live verbs.
Live referent retained in the paddock: foedus is the adopted noun the spec-first spine unit homes in RBS0 (the rbtf_foedus quoin is not yet built).

### one-click avowal via verification_uri_complete — DECLINED for Entra (drained 2026-06-25)

DECLINED for the live IdP: the Entra finding (260617, empirical not just docs) — the live devicecode response from the standing trust omits verification_uri_complete entirely, and it is not a knob (no scope, param, or app-registration toggle turns it on; Microsoft's endpoint structurally lacks it).
Declined fallback recorded: synthesizing a code-carrying URL ourselves (.../device?otc=<code>) is unsupported, Palisade-fragile, rides the device-code-phishing pattern Microsoft's filters flag, and tried live the prefill did not populate.
Revisit trigger: a new or migrated IdP that returns the field, or Microsoft adding it.
