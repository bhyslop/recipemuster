# ₣Bf Heat-Memories — drained paddock deliberation

*Memo — opened 2026-06-23 — provenance home for ideas drained from the ₣Bf (rbk-12-mvp-federation-evolution) paddock as the heat progresses. Experimental practice; see "The drain gate" below.*

## Purpose

The ₣Bf paddock is a holding catalog of federation-evolution ideas. As the heat
progresses, ideas get dispositioned — built into a completed pace, declined, or
superseded — and their deliberation (the named tension, the leans, the options
weighed) no longer belongs in a paddock meant to hold the *shape of remaining
work*. This memo is where that dispositioned deliberation drains, so the paddock
stays focused without the *why* being lost.

Provenance, never authority (the standing memo rule). A drained idea is historical
record: read it freely; if its concern must return, it is re-deliberated into a
fresh pace or spec — never lifted from here as authority.

## What drains here — and what does NOT

Drain ONLY dispositioned ideas:
- **Built** — graduated into a pace that completed; the deliberation is spent and the result lives in code or spec.
- **Declined** — weighed and rejected; recorded with what was considered and why it was set aside.
- **Superseded** — overtaken by a later decision; recorded with the prior framing and what replaced it.

Do NOT drain **deferred-but-still-live** ideas — they stay in the paddock (or move
to an itch / the horizon roadmap if thin). A live idea drained here is a live idea
quietly buried; that is the failure mode to watch.

## The drain gate (experimental, 2026-06-23)

Movement is wrap-considered and groom-judged: when a pace wraps, its wrap decision
considers whether the now-completed work renders some paddock material
dispositioned — but a fact drains here ONLY if a *terminal groom* estimates the
paddock no longer needs it. The groom's judgment, not the wrap alone, releases the
drain.

This practice is being tried and is not obviously going to work well. Revisit if it
adds friction without keeping the paddock clean — or graduate it to a general JJK
practice if it proves out.
