DEBUG3 sshd logging on rocket (enabled via separate OpenSSH/Debug ETW channel, since OpenSSH/Operational only emits Info-level even with LogLevel DEBUG3) confirmed sshd was opening C:\Users\bujuw_user.rocket.003\.ssh\authorized_keys, finding error:2 (file not found), and emitting Failed publickey for the offered key whose fingerprint matched what we'd planted at the canonical C:\Users\bujuw_user\.ssh\authorized_keys.
