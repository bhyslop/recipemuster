Take the delivery candidate the staging heat (₣Bs) proved,
re-cut under this heat's amended ceremony so it sits one commit atop the live public base,
and carry it to a live public release:
stage it on the public repository, walk it as a stranger, promote it to production, and prove the promoted face.
This is the first real content delivery of the federation era — the heat exists so the tectonic step is a sequence of small proven ceremonies, not one leap.
