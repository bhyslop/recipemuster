//axvd_sheaf axd_normative
This is a normative portion of the {jjs0_imprimatur}: binding — code, tests, and decisions may rely on it.

Move a
{jjdpr_pace}
from one
{jjdhr_heat}
to another.
This is a primitive operation: mechanical transfer with no ceremony.
The
{jjdpr_pace}
keeps its
{jjdt_coronet}
— that identity is immutable for life — and is re-affiliated by moving its entry into the destination
{jjdhr_heat};
all
{jjdkr_tack}
history is preserved.

*Important:* "Relocated" is not a
{jjdpr_pace}
state.
{jjdpr_pace}
states remain:
{jjdpe_rough},
{jjdpe_complete},
{jjdpe_abandoned}.
Relocate is an operation that moves
{jjdpr_pace_s}
between
{jjdhr_heat_s};
state is preserved through the move.

{jjds_arguments}

//axd_required
* {jjdt_coronet}
—
{jjdpr_pace}
to move

//axd_required
* {jjda_to}
— destination
{jjdt_firemark}

//axd_optional
* {jjda_before}
— insert before specified
{jjdt_coronet}
in destination

//axd_optional
* {jjda_after}
— insert after specified
{jjdt_coronet}
in destination

//axd_optional
* {jjda_first}
— insert at the first actionable slot in the destination
{jjdhm_order}
(contract at
{jjda_first};
the destination's completed work is never displaced)

*Positioning:* Only one of
{jjda_before},
{jjda_after},
or
{jjda_first}
may be specified.
If none provided, append to end of destination
{jjdhm_order}
(default).

{jjds_stdout} The re-affiliated
{jjdt_coronet}
in destination
{jjdhr_heat}

{jjds_exit_uniform} 0 success, non-zero error.

{jjds_behavior}

.
{jjdr_load};
on failure, exit immediately with
{jjdr_load}
error status
. Resolve the source
{jjdhr_heat}
holding the
{jjdt_coronet}
(resolution as defined at
{jjdt_coronet});
error if no
{jjdhr_heat}
holds it
. Verify destination
{jjdhr_heat}
exists with given
{jjdt_firemark}
. If
{jjda_before}
or
{jjda_after}
provided, verify target
{jjdt_coronet}
exists in destination
{jjdhr_heat}
. Move the
{jjdpr_pace}
entry from source
{jjdhm_paces}
to destination
{jjdhm_paces}
under the same
{jjdt_coronet}
— never re-keyed, never re-minted; all
{jjdkr_tack}
data is carried unchanged
. Insert the
{jjdt_coronet}
into destination
{jjdhm_order}
at determined position
. Remove the
{jjdt_coronet}
from source
{jjdhm_order}
.
{jjdr_save}
. Output the
{jjdt_coronet}
to stdout

*Validation errors:*

[cols="2,3"]
|===
| Condition | Error

| Source and destination same
| "Cannot relocate pace to same heat"

| Source {jjdpr_pace} not found
| "Pace {coronet} not found in any heat"

| Destination {jjdhr_heat} not found
| "Heat {firemark} not found"

| Target {jjdt_coronet} not found in destination
| "Target pace {coronet} not found in heat {firemark}"
|===
