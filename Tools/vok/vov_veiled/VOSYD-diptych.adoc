== Overview

Charter: a
{vosyd_diptych}
is one file per codex holding every sheaf in a word-per-line record grammar —
the canonical source git tracks at word grain —
from which the specification's readable forms are rendered just in time,
and to which agent edits return through a verifying reverse parse.

The
{vosyd_diptych}
replaces the AsciiDoc *expression* of a concept model, never its semantics.
MCM keeps the semantic law (linked terms, categories, exposure, Lapidary);
AXLA keeps the annotation law (voicings, dimensions, lookahead arities, the lintel and its prose);
this sheaf owns only the storage grammar and the machines that project and maintain it.
The
{vosmm_matricula}
is the maintainer: reference↔definition matching, arity validation, tree-nesting
rules, and lintel law all run as census passes over the diptych's structure,
which is designed to be the matricula's native input.

Provenance corpus, cited never restated: the founding vision memo
(`Memos/memo-20260209-diptych-vision.md` — stale in parts, superseded where
this sheaf differs) and the validator feedstock
(`Memos/memo-20260610-quoin-minting-introspection.md` §7).

== The Diptych

The
{vosyd_diptych}
(defined at the cosmology top) is a line-classed record grammar:
the first character(s) of a line determine its class, prose is the unmarked
default, and the only stateful mode is verbatim passthrough.
Sigil characters are provisional until the grammar settles; the record
*classes* are the settled content of this section.

=== Record classes

* *Node* — a hierarchy heading: level plus title, no anchor.
The document is one tree; every other structural record seats in it.
* *Definition node* — a quoin definition: anchor, display text, variant forms
(plural, possessive, past, progressive — enumerated literally), and a seat
kind.
Two seat kinds are preserved for projection fidelity: heading-seated
(rendered as an anchored section title at a level) and dlist-seated
(rendered as an anchored definition-list term under the current node).
A definition node is a hierarchy node; scope is a subtree property.
* *Annotation* — the AXLA voicing line in the direct form only:
primary motif first, dimensions following
(`//axk_premise` shape).
The legacy explicit-relationship form is never stored; encode translates it
on ingestion.
* *Reference token* — a linked-term reference, line-isolated in prose
(the memo's `$`-prefix shape).
* *Backtick token* — the sprue/inlay token stream, lexically distinct from
reference tokens so AXLA's per-stream lookahead counting holds unchanged.
* *Prose word* — one word per line; a trailing punctuation cluster attaches
to its word's line; a leading cluster likewise.
Attachment charsets are a settling-register item.
* *Markup flag* — bold and italic as per-line flags, never spans:
each word in an emphasized run carries the flag; projection joins contiguous
flagged runs into one span.
No toggle state, no delimiter matching.
* *Verbatim fence* — code blocks and tables pass through untokenized under a
kind/language tag until the closing fence.
* *Sheaf boundary* — one record carrying the sheaf's identity, its
normativeness dimension, its genre dimension, and the codex binding.
The sheaf-side lintel and codex-side cartouche of the multi-file world merge
here; their agreement validator dissolves by construction.
The fair-faced opening sentence remains as prose beneath the record, and the
matricula cross-checks sentence against tokens.

The category legend (the mapping-section comment block of the multi-file
world) is deliberately unspecified here — deferred; the
{vosmm_matricula}
sorts legend representation later.

=== What the grammar preserves from AXLA

The annotation layer's positional lookahead grammar carries over verbatim:
an annotation reads typed arguments from the token stream that follows, with
primary-then-dimensions ordering and per-stream counting exactly as AXLA
defines.
Argument tokens stay in the prose, doing double duty as readable sentence and
machine argument; the diptych merely line-isolates them.
Detail-site hierarchy markers become typed nodes in the one tree, and AXLA's
nesting rules become matricula tree validators.
This sheaf mints no annotation vocabulary and changes no arity: AXLA is the
authority, mechanized here.

=== No mapping section

The diptych stores no mapping section.
Display text and variant forms live on the definition node; the matricula
guarantees what the mapping section guaranteed by ceremony —
every reference resolves, every referenced variant is declared, exactly one
definition per
{vosli_inscription}.
The projection synthesizes mapping sections (including foreign-import
attribute lines) because the AsciiDoc form still needs attributes to resolve.

== Codec

Three operations, embodied in the matricula binary (vom) beside the census —
a deliberate widening of that crate's charter from naming census to
census-and-codec, sharing its allowlist substrate and its never-ships posture.
Operation names below are working descriptions, not mints; naming is a
settling-register item.

* *Encode* — one AsciiDoc file tree (codex plus its `include::` sheaves) into
one
{vosyd_diptych}.
Wild-corpus-facing: it claims the recognized MCM/AXLA construct subset and
rejects loud on anything outside it, never best-effort.
Its unclaimed-construct report, run corpus-wide, is the migration survey that
sizes the long tail before any conversion is attempted.
Two ingestion behaviors: legacy voicing lines translate mechanically to the
direct form (drop the relationship token); a lintel or cartouche carrying
only a normativeness token lands on an operator worklist — genre assignment
is judgment, never auto-repair.
* *Emit* — one
{vosyd_diptych}
into the canonical AsciiDoc projection: the multi-file include tree fanned
back out, with mapping sections, cartouches, and lintels synthesized
deterministically from the stored records.
One canonical layout; the projection is a derived artifact, regenerated into
a gitignored derived root, never hand-edited.
* *Prove* — the fidelity gate, per file or corpus-wide:
emit-after-encode must be byte-identical to its own prior projection, and
the asciidoctor rendering of the original source and of the projection must
be equivalent.
There is no separate normalizer: normalization *is* emit∘encode, reviewed by
ordinary git diff, judged by the render-equivalence gate — which is blind to
what normalization touches (comments, attribute ordering, alignment) and
sensitive to what it must not touch (rendered content).

Asciidoctor is a foreign tool at the Palisade: exactly one membrane wraps it,
surfaced both as a step inside prove and as a thin standalone render command.
The pure choice of distribution is the Ruby `asciidoctor` gem invoked as CLI
— the de facto definition of the language until the Eclipse specification
ratifies — version-pinned in the membrane so verdicts are reproducible.

=== The write path

Agent read access is by rendered slice; agent write access returns through
the echo loop:
submitted edited slice → reverse parse → splice into the diptych →
re-render → echo the diff between submitted and regenerated forms for
review.
Semantic loss in the reverse parse is thereby visible at review time.
The reverse parser never faces wild AsciiDoc — the corpus's wildness is
consumed once by migration — so it parses only the canonical projection as
edited by an agent, and an edit that breaks the canonical shape rejects loud
with the offending line, never best-effort.
The slice-addressing grammar and the MCP verb surface are deferred scope;
the echo contract above is the settled part.

== Invariants (rivets deferred)

These propositions are load-bearing; each takes an opaque `VOr_` rivet at
settling, when citing code exists.
Until then they bind as aspirant prose.

* *Canonical source.* The
{vosyd_diptych}
is the sole source of its codex; every AsciiDoc or Markdown form is a
derived, disposable projection.
Post-migration, hand edits land on the diptych (directly or through the echo
loop), never on a projection.
* *Fidelity.* Migration of a file is gated on the prove pass; thereafter emit
is byte-stable and the render-equivalence gate holds continuously.
* *Reject loud.* Encode and the reverse parser fail on the first unclaimed
construct with its location; silent best-effort is forbidden in both
directions.
* *Never ships.* The codec rides the matricula binary and inherits its
distribution boundary (VOr_q4f); no diptych machinery enters the parcel.
* *AXLA unchanged.* The diptych stores AXLA's direct annotation form and
positional lookahead grammar without extension or reinterpretation; a
grammar need discovered here is an AXLA amendment, banked in Reconciliation,
never a local dialect.

== Migration

Per-codex and incremental: one codex converts when its prove pass lands
green, and the multi-file AsciiDoc source for that codex retires in the same
act.
The word-per-line re-flow discharges the removal-conditions ledger the vision
memo carries (semantic-linefeed renormalization among them) as each codex
converts.
Word-grain history begins at conversion; prior ancestry remains in the
retired files' history.
