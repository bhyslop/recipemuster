== Overview

The
{vosmm_matricula}
is a transient census of every
{vosli_inscription}
in use across the repository below the
{voslc_cipher}
tier.
It is an instant temporary database — raised on demand from repository source, queried, and discarded — that subsumes the ad-hoc grepping an agent does to answer naming questions.
It is embodied as a standalone Rust executable — a VOK binary driven by its own tabtarget — that recursively scans the git-tracked project tree.

It serves three jobs the operator asks of the naming system, and a fourth that falls out of the same frozen census:

. Choose an unused
{vosls_signet}
— what is free under a
{voslc_cipher}.
. Prevent a rename from creating a collision — what a proposed
{vosli_inscription}
would clash with.
. Plan a re-mint — what a
{vosls_signet}
currently owns before it is moved.
. Generate the acronym index the `claude-*.md` files maintain by hand today.

The organizing reframe is that every persistent identifier the project mints — what we informally call quoins, sprues, rivets, and filenames — is an
{vosli_inscription}
dressed by a
{voslv_vesture}.
The
{vosmm_matricula}
is therefore one vesture-parameterized scanner, not a family of separate extractors.
Its method is classify-by-subtraction: cast the widest net over `_`- and `-`-shaped tokens, let each known
{voslv_vesture}
claim what it owns, and treat the unclaimed-but-ours remainder — the
{vosme_estray_s}
— as a product rather than waste.

The minting rules the
{vosmm_matricula}
enforces are not defined here.
Terminal-exclusivity, exact collision, and the MCM Lapidary word-selection discipline are CMK/MCM doctrine (CLAUDE.md "Prefix Naming Discipline"); this spec cites them as the authority and the
{vosmm_matricula}
mechanizes them.
This spec mints only the vocabulary the census itself needs.

This spec is the design-time home for the architecture and decomposition below.
Earlier, that knowledge lived in a Job Jockey paddock as a way-station; it is migrated here so it survives the heat that authored it.

== The Matricula

The
{vosmm_matricula}
(defined at the cosmology top) carries the
{vosls_signet}
trie, the
{vosme_estray}
set, and the query indices read by its jobs; it has both structure and behavior, raised by a builder,
{vosms_seal_ed}
into an immutable census, and read through a pure query interface.
This section carries its method.

The
{vosmm_matricula}
is an additive sibling to the existing `vof` library, never a rework of it.
It reuses `vof` as a path dependency: the
{voslc_cipher}
registry (`vofc_registry`) is its input grammar, the release tree-walk (`vofr_collect`) generalizes into its candidate gate, and emission rides the managed-section rail (`voff_freshen`).

=== Census Lifecycle

The
{vosmm_matricula}
is
{axd_petrify}
(AXLA): raised as a mutable Builder, it petrifies on first disclosure into an immutable census, then is queried and discarded.
{vosms_seal}
is the Matricula's name for the petrifying act; the build-then-freeze semantics are AXLA's, cited not restated here.
In Rust the two phases are a typestate — a Builder the scan owns, consumed by
{vosms_seal}
into a frozen struct safe to read concurrently.
The reference shape is the operator's freezable/immutable collection library (`Memos/memo-20260620-freeze-builder-pattern/`, provenance only).

==== Methods

//axhems_scoped_method
=== raise

Construct an empty, mutable Builder over a fixed grammar (the
{voslc_cipher}
registry, the
{voslv_vesture}
set, and the file-selection allowlist).
The grammar is frozen at load; the census it accumulates is not yet.

//axhems_scoped_method
=== seat

Walk the candidate corpus (git-tracked files intersected with the allowlist), tokenize on the widest `_`- and `-`-shaped net, let each
{voslv_vesture}
claim its declarations, and seat every claimed
{vosli_inscription}
into the
{vosls_signet}
trie.
Each unclaimed-but-ours token becomes an
{vosme_estray}.
A second declaration of an already-seated token at a conflicting site is recorded as a collision
{vosmp_presentment}
at seat time.

//axhems_scoped_method
=== {vosms_seal}

Consume the Builder and return the immutable
{vosmm_matricula}.
After
{vosms_seal},
the census accepts queries and no mutation; the seating invariants (terminal-exclusivity, exact collision) are read as properties of the frozen structure.

//axhems_scoped_method
=== render

Produce the operator-facing report — the
{vosmp_presentment_s}
and the
{vosme_estray}
section — and the regenerated acronym index, both pure over the frozen census.

== Invariants

The three propositions below are load-bearing.
Each is defined here once under an opaque `VOr_` identifier and cited from code and tests by that identifier; the rationale lives here and is never restated beside the marker (ACG "residue", MCM `mcm_rivet`).

[[VOr_k3p]]
//axvc_dictum
*Transient by design.*
A
{vosmm_matricula}
has no persistent existence.
It lives only for the call that summons it, is re-derived from source each time, and is discarded.
Reading the whole tree is instant relative to an agent's grep laps, so nothing is stored — only the grammar (the small, stable
{voslc_cipher}
and
{voslv_vesture}
set) is maintained.
Transience is what defers the heavier maintained-registry layer; a future maintained census would supersede this invariant deliberately, never by drift.

[[VOr_m7w]]
//axvc_dictum
*Mechanical, never linguistic.*
The
{vosmm_matricula}
performs set operations over
{vosli_inscription_s}
and never generates words, fuzzy-matches, or interprets meaning.
It reports the constraint space; the operator supplies the meaning.
A consequence binds the
{vosmp_presentment}:
a
{voslc_cipher}
-gate that trusts foreign namespaces is fallible, so sprue and rivet checks stay advisory, never assertions (see Seating Validators).

[[VOr_q4f]]
//axvc_dictum
*Never ships.*
The
{vosmm_matricula}
is operator-only infrastructure and must never enter the distributed substrate.
The boundary stands on four independent guards: its source is not collected (VOK is unmanaged and the source is
{vosp_veiled}
), its behavior is not linked into the shipped `vvx` (never a `vof`/`vvr` dependency), and its built binary is never committed (it lands in a gitignored `target/release/` under the veil).
Inclusion would require a deliberate, auditable act — a new managed-kit entry, a new `vvr` dependency, or an explicit copy into the parcel — never a silent compile flag.
This is the whole reason the runtime is a standalone binary rather than a feature-stripped subcommand of a shipped tool.

== Decomposition

Four tiers, with the
{vosms_seal}
line falling between Tier 1 and Tier 2.

Tier 0 — Grammar (maintained, frozen at load): the
{voslc_cipher}
registry (VOF, exists), the
{voslv_vesture}
set (the Liturgy, exists), and the file-selection allowlist (new — an explicit allowlist in a distinct file, `.md`/`.adoc`/`.rs`/`.sh` at minimum, recursive, narrowed by a git-tracked-only gate and the cipher/veiled gate).
A cross-kit direction is recorded (260705, from the ₣Br census-pace experience): the allowlist is anticipated as a projection of JJ's *tackle* table — the scope→discipline binding of the aspirant tackle sheaf (`Tools/jjk/vov_veiled/JJS-aspirant-tackle.adoc`, its representation fork) — read by a shared substrate crate so JJ dispatch and this census select files from one table.
The discipline a glob carries implies its
{voslv_vesture}
(the guides are what keep declaration sites pattern-clean), and the file-role layer (Scan Mechanics) is the same axis at scan grain.
Until the tackle representation settles, the allowlist is shaped schema-compatible with that table and homed VOK-side.

Tier 1 — Raising the census (mutable Builder): walk -> tokenize -> classify-by-subtraction -> seat each
{vosli_inscription}
into the
{vosls_signet}
trie; then
{vosms_seal}.

Tier 2 — The frozen
{vosmm_matricula}
(immutable): the
{vosls_signet}
trie, the
{vosme_estray}
set, and the query indices.

Tier 3 — Reading it (pure over the frozen census): the MVP validators and the report render plus acronym-index generation.

The parts are mostly allocation, not new minting.
{vosli_inscription},
{vosls_signet},
{voslv_vesture},
{voslc_cipher},
and
{vosle_epithet}
are existing Liturgy quoins, reused by reference.
Only three quoins are new vo work:
{vosme_estray},
{vosmp_presentment},
and
{vosms_seal}.

== Classify by Subtraction

The ours-or-foreign gate is the project
{voslc_cipher},
invariant across the `_` and `-` separators.
Each known
{voslv_vesture}
claims the declarations it owns; whatever carries our
{voslc_cipher}
yet no
{voslv_vesture}
claims is an
{vosme_estray}
(defined at the cosmology top) — reported in its own section, never folded into the validator
{vosmp_presentment_s}.

== Seating Validators

The MVP validators fall out of the
{vosms_seal}
invariants rather than being separate passes.
Both are mechanical, honoring the never-linguistic invariant (VOr_m7w).

*Exact collision* is the frozen-map "same key, different value" conflict observed at seat time: two distinct declarations of one token.
It is a count-blind property — two declaration sites, never an occurrence count.

*Terminal-exclusivity* is a
{vosls_signet}
that is both seated and has children: a property of a trie node read after
{vosms_seal}.
The rule itself is CMK/MCM doctrine; this validator mechanizes it.

Each validator emits a
{vosmp_presentment}
(defined at the cosmology top) — a formal report of one minting violation the census observed directly, carrying the violating
{vosli_inscription_s},
the declaration sites, and the violated rule cited to its CMK/MCM home.
Per VOr_m7w, a
{vosmp_presentment}
over a fallible gate (sprue, rivet) is advisory, not an assertion.

This
{vosmp_presentment}
set widens VOS0's
{vosk_prefix_validation},
which validates prefix-derivation for *file names only* at release time.
The
{vosmm_matricula}
generalizes that narrow, release-time, filename gate to the whole-environment declaration census across every
{voslv_vesture}.

== Lintel Validators

Two validators read the sheaf/codex declaration pair — the sheaf-side lintel (AXLA
`axl_lintel`: the `axvd_sheaf` voicing line plus its opening sentence) and the codex-side
cartouche — rather than the
{vosls_signet}
trie.
Both are mechanical, honoring the never-linguistic invariant (VOr_m7w), and both cite their
doctrine home rather than restating it (Doctrine Homes below).
They ride the same walk and frozen census as the seating validators; they are listed apart
from the MVP delivery, whose definition stands unchanged.

*Attestation agreement* is the cartouche↔lintel cross-check AXLA anticipates
(`axvd_cartouche`): the dimension tokens a codex attests at an `include::` site must equal
the tokens the included sheaf's lintel declares.
A drifted pair emits a
{vosmp_presentment}
carrying both sites.

*Timeless prose* mechanizes AXLA's Timeless Prose law (`axl_timeless`) — the skidmark
detector: a date-shaped token in the body of a sheaf whose lintel carries `axd_normative`,
`axd_executory`, or `axd_informative` emits a
{vosmp_presentment}
citing the law, honoring its two allowances (an `axd_survey` sheaf is exempt entire; a date
embedded in a memo path is a reference, never a mark on the prose) and its exemption of
`axd_aspirant`/`axd_notional` sheaves.
Detection is a date-shaped-token pattern over sheaf bodies with lintel-token context — exact
and structural, no meaning interpreted.

== Report and Acronym Index

The MVP delivery is a whole-environment scan run as a standalone VOK executable through its own tabtarget, with no MCP surface.
It reports the
{vosmp_presentment_s}
(the two minting violations) and the
{vosme_estray}
section, and it regenerates the acronym index from the same frozen census rather than leaving it hand-maintained.

The report is execution-time output to a terminal (ACG three-homes: execution-time).
It carries no sprue, because it is not a wire format.
Should a downstream consumer ever parse a structured census output, that output becomes an RB-authored wire format and its keys must carry a sprue (ACG fourth clause) — but that consumer and its envelope are out of MVP scope (see Deferred Scope).

== Scan Mechanics

The scan distinguishes declaration sites from reference sites; this axis, not name-resolution, is what the validators ride.
A file bears declarations, references, or both: code (`.rs`/`.sh`) and specs (`.adoc`) bear both; Memos and prose `.md` bear references only.
Collision and terminal-exclusivity validate declarations; references feed later interrogations (out of MVP scope).

Declaration recognition is structural and per-
{voslv_vesture}
— each
{voslv_vesture}
carries its own declaration-site recognizer (`fn prefix_…`, `prefix_…() {`, `[[anchor]]`) — never whole-program name-resolution.
There is no semantic analyzer: the hard part of language tooling is resolution, which the census does not need, and the transience invariant (VOr_k3p) prices a per-call semantic build out of the "instant" budget.
The tooling ladder is per-
{voslv_vesture}
line patterns for the MVP (the codebase's RCG/BCG discipline keeps declaration sites pattern-clean), with tree-sitter adopted incrementally where a
{voslv_vesture}
needs real structure — never syn (rust-only).

Correctness rests on the grep-as-registry axiom: a minted name must be textually present at its declaration.
A name synthesized by a macro (`paste!`/`concat_idents!`) or bash `eval` is invisible to a structural scan; a referenced-but-never-declared token is therefore itself a
{vosmp_presentment},
not something to chase with an expander.
RCG and BCG already forbid identifier-synthesis, which is what makes this axiom safe to lean on.

A file-role layer sits atop the allowlist and git-tracked gate: a path is declaration-bearing, reference-only, index-of-record (the `claude-*.md` acronym tables the MVP regenerates), or generated.
Memos are the first reference-only case — excluded from the MVP declaration scan, with a removal condition: they re-enter as a reference corpus when reference-tracing lands later, because a memo citing an old name is a reference a rename should know about.

== Deferred Scope

The MVP is the scan plus the two seating validators plus the
{vosme_estray}
report plus acronym-index generation.
The concept widens later, and that widening is out of scope for this spec:

* The richer interrogations — what-owns / what-is-free / who-references.
* Safe-reprefix — the cross-repo-contained rename apex, legal only within a
{vosls_signet}
's single writable home.
* The engagement determinations — when an ambient check runs, when a gate blocks.
* The gestalt-label subsystem.
* A maintained (non-transient) census, which would deliberately supersede VOr_k3p.
* The grimoire — a maintained, flat table of the human-presentation surface forms (every plural, every verb tense, the possessive) of the words a minted
{vosli_inscription}
dresses, read by the
{vosmm_matricula}
to reach the word-surface layer the identifier scan cannot.

Each is named here to bound the MVP; none is specified here — save the grimoire, whose shape gelled enough in design to sketch below, though it stays deferred and unminted, with no
{vosli_inscription}
status of its own yet.

=== Grimoire (deferred sketch)

The grimoire is the design-time home for the human-presentation forms of a minted word — one flat row per word that surfaces in prose.
A row enumerates its forms literally — `foedus`, `foedera`, `foedus's` — never as a stemming or derivation rule: the irregular forms cannot be derived, only known.
This is the freeze pattern in linguistic form — the forms are authored once with full wisdom (the linguistic act, a mind's), then read forever by exact match (the mechanical act).
The
{vosmm_matricula}
never stems or interprets at scan time, so the grimoire keeps VOr_m7w whole: it reports the constraint space, and the operator supplies the meaning.

It serves three jobs, each a reach into word-surface space the identifier scan cannot make:

. Lint prose for a missed citation — a word standing in plain prose where it ought to be a linked-term reference to its
{vosli_inscription}.
. Mechanize the Lapidary word-selection gate — at mint time, test a candidate word and its every inflection against the seated forms, the collision check the MVP leaves to manual grep (CLAUDE.md "Prefix Naming Discipline").
. Generate the human-presentation string constants for Rust and bash, then lint printed strings against them — error output is operator-facing (ashlar), where word-care bites hardest.

The distribution boundary holds as for the acronym index: the grimoire's output (the generated constants) may ride in shipped code, but the grimoire and the
{vosmm_matricula}
that reads it never ship (VOr_q4f).
Rows stay flat and independent — any relationship a later job wants (the asterism, say) enters as a single categorical tag on the row, never a row-to-row edge, until a real traversal earns the graph.
A worked census (the ₣Br mint pace, 260705) sharpened the sketch with field evidence — it ran this gate by hand roughly twenty times against the seating validators' once, the frequency fact for where the operator's word-labor actually lives:

* The completeness tension resolves by making the gate self-maintaining: the gate's input IS an authored row — the candidate word and its forms, the linguistic act a mind performs at probe time — and adoption appends that row to the table.
  The gate tool and the table maintainer are one act; a rejected candidate's row is discarded with its verdict ledgered.
  (This supersedes the mint-time-hook lean the sketch previously recorded.)
* The prose lint (job 1) carries two verdict branches: a hit is a missed citation (enquoin it) or an *innocent homograph* — ordinary-English use of a minted word — to be reworded or formally accepted.
  Detection is exact-match over row forms (VOr_m7w holds); the branch verdict is meaning, and stays the operator's.
* Minting is retroactive: a new row owes a one-time sweep of existing prose and strings, because yesterday's innocent prose becomes today's homograph the moment the word mints.
  The backlog debuts per-row at mint time, never as a big-bang lint.
* Accepted homographs need a durable record or every scan re-flags them; site-pinned waivers rot, so the lean is acceptance-by-domain carried on the row.
  Open, recorded.
* A make-room job joins the three: given a word, report its weighted footprint (code identifiers > wire keys > spec quoins > prose) — the eviction-cost census behind "free this excellent word for a better home" — and its ambient form, the low-footprint inventory of eviction-cheap seats.
  Footprint advises cost, never verdicts (the pin→counterfoil eviction proceeded at ~245 uses; the billet resolution moved a 10-hit incumbent): value is the operator's judgment, and the execute half is authorship — each prose site needs a written sentence — permanently manual, never a safe-reprefix sibling.
* The register tag earns its keep immediately: list-by-register is the coffer census, and flat tags sufficed across the whole census pace — no edge was ever needed.

Promotion of this sketch to an aspirant of its own is a later parceling, taken when the
{vosmm_matricula}
itself ascends — not here.
