The
{rbtt_theurge}
is Recipe Bottle's test-orchestration engine: it composes the project's fixtures
(BUS0 `busr_fixture`) into named suites (`busr_suite`) and runs them against real
regimes, real cloud, and real crucibles.
This subdoc treats the cosmology the engine embodies — the vocabulary code cites,
defined with the rest of the codex's terms above — and defers every containment,
membership, and identity fact to its production home.

== The wrapper(inner) combinator

A runnable `busr_suite` is, in the theurge model, exactly one product:
one set of inner bodies run under one
{rbtt_wrapper}.
The inner bodies are the suite's
{rbtt_case_s};
the wrapper is the substrate posture they run against.
The suite-composition oracle (`rbtdtc_crucible.rs`) proves every literal suite in
`RBTDRA_SUITES` remains expressible as this wrapper(inner) product, and asserts the
structural laws the product implies.

== The three strata

A
{rbtt_stratum}
is a dependency tier — an RB instance of the BUS0 `busr_suite` dependency-tier axis
— gating which fixtures a suite may host by what external dependency they need.

== Suite membership

The membership of every named suite — which fixtures compose reveille, picket,
gauntlet, and the rest — is owned by the hand-written `RBTDRA_SUITES` literal
registry in `rbtdra_almanac.rs`, the compile-checked source of truth.
It is not transcribed here (ACG: reference the home, don't recreate): a member list
restated in prose drifts silently from the registry.
`rbtdtc_crucible.rs` is the oracle proving each literal list stays expressible as a
wrapper(inner) product.

== The standing freehold subject

The rig pins exactly one standing freehold subject —
`RBTDGC_FREEHOLD_SUBJECT`, the operator's Entra `oid`, homed in RBPC
(`rbpc_constants.sh`).
In the production identity-layers model this is the PERMANENT, pool-independent
citizen-definition layer, and it alone is the test rig's to pin.
That model — the permanent
{rbtf_citizen}
definition versus the EVOLVING
{rbtf_foedus}
and depot instances a live admission is scoped to — is defined production-side
(RBSRF); this subdoc pins the one subject and references the rest.

== The parley admission probe

*parley* — the word RBSTC reserved for the federation authentic-verb fixture — has
graduated: it is a built, base-free probe suite (`rbtdrv_patrol.rs`) running under the
{rbtt_reuse}, proving the POSITIVE federation-admission round-trip against the standing
{rbtt_freehold}
subject.
It drives the real polity verbs — an unseat, then a restore brevet, on the subject's
retriever mantle — and asserts through rehearse's depot-attributed manor roll (RBSPO)
that the muniment stands at baseline, vanishes after the unseat, and stands again after
the restore, then leaves the freehold exactly as found via a final don-green poll over
the eventually-consistent IAM binding.
Every roll assertion is an exact (depot, mantle, subject) line scoped to the
freehold's own depot: identical records co-reside
across polity slices — orphans of unmade depots persist in the payor-grain terrier,
since churn runs no terrier sweep — so a depot-blind assertion cannot attribute an
aliasing line at all.
The live run that forced the depot ruling proved the attribution's diagnostic worth the
same day: attributed, its alias resolved to a same-depot muniment-schema skew, which the
depot-blind roll had misread as a cross-depot orphan.
It is the positive mirror of its denial-band sibling, which owns every rejection-band
assertion; parley makes no denial poll of its own.
The fixture also rides the picket
{rbtt_stratum}
(and echelon); its membership, like every suite's, is owned by `RBTDRA_SUITES`, not
transcribed here.
