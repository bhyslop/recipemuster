NOTE: This operation is the once-per-cycle guided reveal: it walks the
operator through the disclosure of the standing dry
{rbth_candidate},
performing every assert itself while every push is typed by the operator —
the machine-asserts-around-human-pushes law of the cosmology's command seam.
It never re-cuts: the dry candidate is bit-for-bit what ships, and a terminal
re-cut would break exactly that identity.

NOTE: *Laps and reveal are decoupled in time.* Days may pass between the dry
{rbth_lap}
and the reveal, so the candidate's freshness is asserted, never presumed —
delivered-bytes-bearing drift since the dry cut sends the cycle back to laps,
never forward.

NOTE: *Gate ownership.* The
{rbth_quarantine}
gates live here, relocated from the lap path: a
{rbth_lap}
involves no quarantine, and the reveal will not proceed onto a quarantine
that is visible to strangers or carrying an undispositioned prior cut.
