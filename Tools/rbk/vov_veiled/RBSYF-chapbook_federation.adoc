== Overview

This chapbook tells the federation tale:
how a human comes to act inside a
{rbtgi_depot}
with no key anywhere and every act attributed —
from the manor's founding trust,
through admission onto a mantle,
to the audit line that names the acting service account and the human behind it together —
and how holdings are withdrawn when the story runs its shadow side.

The tale binds stitching only:
a cast of actors quartered into venues, and an ordered run of interactions.
Every parameter, output, guarantee, and rejection band lives in the cited contracts, never here.
The marker skeleton is the truth;
any rendered diagram — its boxes, its arrows, its inscriptions — is derived presentation.

*Oracle.*
The story spine is three fixtures.
The freehold-establish arc
(`Tools/rbk/rbtd/src/rbtdrk_depot.rs`, the `RBTDRK_CASES_FREEHOLD_ESTABLISH` registry)
walks the positive founding in registered order: levy, avowal, gird, the brevet-and-don pairs, and the founding-whole recognosce.
The parley fixture
(`Tools/rbk/rbtd/src/rbtdrv_patrol.rs`, `RBTDRV_CASES_PARLEY`)
proves the withdrawal round-trip on the manor roll — the muniment stands, vanishes, stands.
Polity-denial
(same file, `RBTDRV_CASES_POLITY_DENIAL`)
pins the shadow side's exact rejection bands and the per-mantle isolation of admission.
An interaction that contradicts a passing case is wrong — the fixture, not the chapbook, is authoritative.
The tale compresses where the oracle repeats:
each mechanism is told once, and an interaction notes the cases it compresses.

*Stage geography.*
Identity originates at the
{rbtf_census} —
the external IdP that alone says who exists —
and flows through the manor's one workforce pool
({rbrw_regime})
and a per-foedus provider
({rbrf_regime})
into a depot,
where the three
{rbtf_mantle_s}
stand as the only actors resources ever see.
Who holds what is recorded beside the trust:
the
{rbtf_terrier}
keeps one
{rbtf_muniment}
per holding, and the record moves before the power, both directions.
No keyfile exists at any point of the flow;
what replaces possession is the
{rbsk_human_present}
premise — a live human sign-in behind every mantle token.

== The Tale

=== Movement I — The Manor Founded

Oracle: none of the spine's cases —
this movement is the ground the oracles presume:
parley and polity-denial baseline-assert against a standing manor, and freehold-establish levies into one.

//axhci_interaction
. {rbtr_payor} {rbtgi_manor} — via
{rbtgo_manor_instaurate},
the payor founds the manor's scriptable substrate:
the one workforce pool ensured by list-and-match against its committed record
({rbrw_regime})
and the terrier bucket —
ensure-exists, idempotent, never destructive
(the depot-grain polity folder is founded at depot levy).

//axhci_interaction axd_attended axd_foreign
. {rbtr_payor} {rbtf_census} — at the census's own console,
the payor registers the application that will assert citizens:
the guided walk whose yield is a foedus's core trust values,
landing in the committed federation regime
({rbrf_regime}).
No operation of ours governs a neighbor's console;
the walk is a handbook track without a citable home — see Surfaced Gaps.

//axhci_interaction
. {rbtr_payor} {rbtf_foedus} — via
{rbtgo_manor_affiance},
the payor pledges the trust:
a provider seated under the standing pool, with its attribute mapping,
its body conditional on the foedus's acquisition mechanism
({rbrf_mechanism}).
A ceremony with founding gravity, once per foedus;
the pool it hangs under must already stand.

=== Movement II — The Polity Levied

Oracle: `rbtdrk_freehold_ensure`, `rbtdrk_depot_recognosce`.
A depot rises inside the founded manor, federation-ready from birth.

//axhci_interaction

{rbtgo_depot_levy},
the payor levies the depot:
project, repository, mason service account — and the three
{rbtf_mantle_s},
their resource authority granted once here and frozen,
with the registry's Data-Access audit trail enabled at birth.
The oracle reuses a standing freehold when one is active; the levy is the create path it proves.

//axhci_interaction

{rbtgo_depot_recognosce},
the founding is recognosced:
a read-only proof that the mantles, their capability-sets, and the audit config stand whole.
The tale tells it beside the levy it verifies;
the oracle holds this case to the arc's very end,
pronouncing the founding whole only after every admission below has ridden on it.

=== Movement VI — The Withdrawal Rehearsed

Oracle: `rbtdrv_parley` and `rbtdrv_polity_denial`.
Suspension is everyday and cheap, and the record moves first in both directions.

//axhci_interaction
. {rbtr_governor} {rbtf_terrier} — via
{rbtgo_terrier_rehearse},
the governor rehearses the manor roll:
the baseline — the citizen's retriever
{rbtf_muniment}
stands, attributed to its admitting foedus.

//axhci_interaction
. {rbtr_governor} {rbtf_mantle} — via
{rbtgo_citizen_unseat},
the governor unseats the citizen from the retriever mantle:
the muniment expunged first, then the `tokenCreator` binding withdrawn —
suspension, not erasure; the depot-scoped binding stands.
Polity-denial pins the consequence:
the retriever don now refuses in the exact admission band — never bare failure —
while the held mantles still don clean: admission is per-mantle, by proof.

//axhci_interaction
. {rbtr_governor} {rbtf_terrier} — via
{rbtgo_terrier_rehearse},
the roll no longer holds the muniment —
the expunge is immediate in the strongly-consistent terrier.

//axhci_interaction
. {rbtr_governor} {rbtf_mantle} — via
{rbtgo_citizen_brevet},
the governor restores the brevet — a genuinely fresh engross;
the impersonation binding settles eventually,
and the oracle polls the retriever don back to green
so the freehold leaves exactly as it was found.

//axhci_interaction
. {rbtr_governor} {rbtf_terrier} — via
{rbtgo_terrier_rehearse},
the muniment stands again:
stands, vanishes, stands — the round-trip the parley suite exists to prove.

Beneath the verbs, polity-denial also pins the record layer itself:
the terrier sub-operations
({rbtf_engross},
{rbtf_expunge},
{rbtf_peruse})
are each driven under the fault seam on a synthetic subject,
so the record's refusals reject in bands as exact as the admission's.

== Surfaced Gaps

Story-first authoring exists to surface exactly these.
Both gaps are graduation conditions for this chapbook, alongside its cast-and-interaction ratification.

*The access arc is uncatalogued.*
{rbtf_avow},
{rbtf_don},
and the sitting lifecycle around them
({rbtf_sitting},
{rbtf_novate},
{rbtf_espy})
are civic definitions carrying real normative weight,
and the accessor surface is implemented and enrolled —
the federated-access check, the mantle-access check that writes the attributed audit entry,
the read-only attribution-trail display, and the sitting probes —
yet none of them stands behind an operation anchor voiced for citation,
where every admission and manor verb does.
Movements III and V therefore carry the avow, don, and attributed-action interactions licensed
(attended or foreign) rather than cited — licensed, but naming a real gap.
The breach dissolves when the access operation quoins and sheaves are minted and the interactions cite them.

*The IdP-console walk is guide-only.*
The payor's application registration at the census —
the guided walk yielding a foedus's core trust values —
has a handbook track but no catalogued home to cite;
Movement I carries it attended and foreign.
The foreignness is permanent (a neighbor's console is never ours to legislate);
the citable guide home is what is missing.
