//axhob_operation
{rbtgo_citizen_brevet}

NOTE: This operation admits a
{rbtf_citizen}
onto a
{rbtf_mantle}
in the
{rbtgi_depot}
— the
{rbtf_brevet}
act, the everyday admission verb. It is a thin idempotent composition over the
terrier muniment sub-ops and two IAM bindings, written muniment-first: the
`tokenCreator` binding on the mantle
{giam_service_account}
(naming a `principal://` member — the don grant) and, on the citizen's first
admission to the depot, the `serviceUsageConsumer` binding on the
{rbtgi_depot_project}
(the Leg-3 quota project). First-vs-further admission differs only in whether the
depot-scoped binding is already present; the idempotent ensure absorbs the difference.

NOTE: The
{rbtr_governor}
performs the everyday brevet, wielding the donned governor
{rbtf_mantle}.
The citizen's federated workforce subject arrives as the param1 folio; the mantle to
admit onto is the second positional argument (`governor` | `director` | `retriever`).
The composition body is token-agnostic, so the same core serves two payor-credentialed
paths: the founding-exception brevet — the
{rbtr_payor}
breveting the first governor inside the levy, the one admission outside governor
wielding — and the interim admission proof.

NOTE: *Intent-first ordering.* The
{rbtf_muniment}
is engrossed before any IAM binding. A crash after the muniment but before a binding
leaves a visible deficit — a recorded holding without its grant — corrected by
re-running brevet (idempotent); never a silent surplus.

NOTE: *Two distinct canonical IAM paths* (BCG Interface Contamination). The
mantle-SA `tokenCreator` binding names a `principal://` member through the SA-scope
principal grant, never the `serviceAccount:` member sibling; the depot
`serviceUsageConsumer` binding uses the project-scope grant with that same
`principal://` member verbatim. The role IDs are the named constants
`RBGC_ROLE_IAM_SERVICE_ACCOUNT_TOKEN_CREATOR` and
`RBGC_ROLE_SERVICEUSAGE_SERVICE_USAGE_CONSUMER` (the Canonical Role IDs block).

//axhos_step

{rbtf_muniment}*

.. {rbtf_engross}
a muniment recording (subject, mantle) into the polity's terrier folder
.. NOTE: the engross carries the atomic, server-adjudicated write-semantics of the

//axhos_step

{giam_service_account}*
(the don grant):
.. ensure `«PRINCIPAL»` holds `RBGC_ROLE_IAM_SERVICE_ACCOUNT_TOKEN_CREATOR` on
`«MANTLE_EMAIL»` via the SA-scope principal grant — an idempotent read-modify-write,
an already-present member a no-op
.. {rbbc_require}
the binding present
.. NOTE: this is the grant that lets the citizen, having donned, mint a short-lived
mantle token (Leg 3) against this mantle.

//axhos_step

{rbtgi_depot_project}*
(first admission per depot):
.. {rbtoe_iam_grant_project}
on the
{rbtgi_depot_project}
... Add `«PRINCIPAL»` with `RBGC_ROLE_SERVICEUSAGE_SERVICE_USAGE_CONSUMER`
.. {rbbc_require}
the binding present
.. NOTE: Leg 3 names the depot as the quota project (`x-goog-user-project`); this
binding is what lets the principal use it. A further admission to another mantle in
the same depot finds it already present — the idempotent ensure is what makes
first-vs-further admission differ only at this binding.
