//axhob_operation
{rbtgo_depot_recognosce}

NOTE: This operation recognosces the
{rbtgi_depot}
founding — a read-only inspection confirming the three
{rbtf_mantle_s}
stand with their full capability-sets and that the Artifact Registry Data-Access audit config is in force.
It re-reads live state from a known, established
{rbtgi_depot}
rather than witnessing the
{rbtgo_depot_levy}
that created it, so it verifies the founding as it actually stands and reruns in isolation.
The operation mutates nothing; a missing piece is fatal and names what is absent.

//axhos_step
. *Authenticate as
{rbtr_payor}*:
.. Execute
{rbtoe_payor_authenticate}
.. {rbbc_store}
`«PAYOR_TOKEN»` from the resulting access token

//axhos_step
. *Confirm the three {rbtf_mantle_s} exist*:
.. For each `«ROLE»` in `governor`, `director`, `retriever`, the mantle email is `${RBCC_account_mantle_«ROLE»}@${RBGD_SA_EMAIL_FULL}`:
... {rbbc_call}
link:https://cloud.google.com/iam/docs/reference/rest/v1/projects.serviceAccounts/get[iam.projects.serviceAccounts.get] with:
.... method: `GET`
.... endpoint: `https://iam.googleapis.com/v1/projects/{rbtgi_depot_project}/serviceAccounts/«mantle email»`
.... header: `Authorization: Bearer «PAYOR_TOKEN»`
... {rbbc_require}
HTTP 200 — the mantle SA is present; a 404 is fatal, naming the absent mantle

//axhos_step
. *Read the project IAM policy; require the project-scoped capability-set bindings and the audit config*:
.. {rbbc_call}
link:https://cloud.google.com/resource-manager/reference/rest/v3/projects/getIamPolicy[cloudresourcemanager.googleapis.com/v3/projects:getIamPolicy]
with body `{"options":{"requestedPolicyVersion":3}}` (v3 is load-bearing — `auditConfigs` are returned only at v3)
.. {rbbc_store}
`«PROJECT_POLICY»` from the response
.. {rbbc_require}
for each mantle, every *project-scoped* binding its capability-set grants is present in `«PROJECT_POLICY».bindings[]` for member `serviceAccount:«mantle email»`.
The authoritative per-role lists are the capability-set functions `rbgw_grant_governor_capabilities`, `rbgw_grant_director_capabilities`, and `rbgw_grant_retriever_capabilities` (this spec names the scope, not the binding inventory — see those functions to avoid drift).
At project scope that is: governor `roles/owner`; retriever `roles/artifactregistry.reader` and `roles/containeranalysis.occurrences.viewer`; director `roles/cloudbuild.builds.editor`, `roles/viewer`, and `roles/cloudbuild.workerPoolUser`.
.. {rbbc_require}
`«PROJECT_POLICY».auditConfigs[]` carries the `artifactregistry.googleapis.com` service with both `ADMIN_READ` and `DATA_READ` log types — the founding audit config established by
{rbtgo_depot_levy}

//axhos_step
. *Read the resource-scoped policies; require the director's off-project capability-set bindings*:
.. The director capability-set spans three resources beyond the project; confirm each via its own `getIamPolicy`:
... {rbbc_call}
`getIamPolicy` on the
{rbtgi_depot}
Artifact Registry repository; {rbbc_require} the director mantle holds `roles/artifactregistry.repoAdmin`
... {rbbc_call}
`getIamPolicy` on the
{rbtr_mason}
service account; {rbbc_require} the director mantle holds `roles/iam.serviceAccountUser` (actAs on
{rbtr_mason})
... {rbbc_call}
`getIamPolicy` on the director mantle's own service account; {rbbc_require} the director mantle holds `roles/iam.serviceAccountUser` on itself (self-actAs)

//axhoc_completion
Successful execution confirms, against live GCP, that the
{rbtgi_depot}
founding stands whole: all three
{rbtf_mantle_s}
exist, each carries every binding of its capability-set across the project, the Artifact Registry repository, and the relevant service-account policies, and the Artifact Registry Data-Access audit config is in force.
The operation is read-only; it creates and modifies nothing.
Any absent mantle, missing binding, or absent audit entry is fatal and names the specific piece — exit 0 means, and only means, the founding is whole.
