`rbsyp`

== Overview

This chapbook tells the provenance tale:
how a container image comes to be trusted inside a
{rbsc_crucible} —
from the capture of the tools that will build it,
through the four construction modes,
to the vouched pull that ends at a nameplate's hallmark line —
and how the trail closes when a hallmark dies.

The tale binds stitching only:
a cast of actors quartered into venues, and an ordered run of interactions,
with each cloud submission bound to the fact that rides home as one vertical group.
Every parameter, output, guarantee, and rejection band lives in the cited contracts, never here.
The marker skeleton is the truth;
any rendered diagram — its boxes, its activation bars, its arrow legends — is derived presentation.

*Oracle.*
The story spine is the onboarding-sequence fixture
(`Tools/rbk/rbtd/src/rbtdro_onboarding.rs`, the `RBTDRO_CASES_ONBOARDING_SEQUENCE` registry):
eight cases in registered order, walking the operator-facing onboarding tracks for every vessel-construction mode.
An interaction that contradicts a passing case is wrong — the fixture, not the chapbook, is authoritative.
The tale compresses where the oracle repeats:
each mechanism is told once, and a movement notes the cases it compresses.

*Stage geography.*
The registry venue holds two lanes:
the capture side
({gar_lodes_namespace}),
where upstream material is impounded into
{rbtga_lode_s},
and the release side
({gar_hallmarks_namespace}),
where built images stand as
{rbtga_ark_s},
each set under a datestamped
{rbtga_hallmark}.
Everything the crucible will ever run has crossed from the first lane to the second under a contract told below.

== The Tale

Oracle: `rbtdro_onboarding_conclave_reliquary`.
Before anything is built, the tools that build are themselves captured.

//axhci_interaction

{rbtgo_lode_conclave}

minting the reliquary Lode and sealing its
{rbtga_lode_vouch}.

{rbtgo_lode_conclave},

{rbtga_touchmark}

=== Movement II — The Local Kludge

Oracle: `rbtdro_onboarding_kludge_tadmor`, `rbtdro_onboarding_kludge_ccyolo`.
The development-loop mode: build on the station, no cloud in the path.

//axhci_interaction
. `rbsyp_director` `rbsyp_vessel` — via
{rbtgo_ark_kludge},
the director builds a nameplate's sentry and bottle vessels locally;
each build mints a local
{rbtga_hallmark}.

//axhci_interaction
. `rbsyp_director` `rbsyp_nameplate` — via
{rbtgo_nameplate_drive} —
composed one-shot by the kludge itself —
each fresh hallmark lands in the consuming nameplate's
{rbrn_regime}
hallmark line, and is committed.

//axhci_interaction
. `rbsyp_director` `rbsyp_vessel` — the director anoints the graft vessel:
reading the bottle kludge's chained build facts,
the anoint rewrites the graft vessel's `RBRV_GRAFT_IMAGE` to the freshly kludged image ref, and the operator commits it.
The grafted image's provenance begins here, on the station.

The anoint interaction above cites no governing contract because none is catalogued;
the breach is deliberate and recorded under Surfaced Gaps.

== Surfaced Gaps

Story-first authoring exists to surface exactly these.
Both gaps are graduation conditions for this chapbook, alongside its cast-and-interaction ratification.

*Anoint is uncatalogued.*
Its
{rbch_enchase}
siblings —
{rbtgo_director_feoff},
{rbtgo_director_yoke},
{rbtgo_nameplate_drive} —
are quoined with operation sheaves;
anoint is implemented and enrolled (the graft-vessel anoint verb) yet appears in the codex only as a bare word.
Movement II's anoint interaction therefore stands uncited, in deliberate breach of the chapbook covenant.
The breach dissolves when the anoint operation quoin and sheaf are minted and the interaction cites them.

*The airgap base handoff has no verb.*
The forge-hallmark locator enters the airgap vessel's
{rbrv_image_anchor}
by direct write — the oracle writes it in orchestration; an operator hand-edits.
Movement IV carries it as an attended, quoinless interaction — licensed, but naming a real gap:
the hallmark-namespace base-provisioning mechanism has no operator verb or contract home,
where its Lode-locator sibling has
{rbtgo_director_feoff}.
